Te Whare Tapa Wh훮 is about approaching your health & wellbeing from multiple areas, and thinking of them as the pillars of your home. You need to have multiple strong walls or pillars to keep the whare standing just as you do to keep yourself in good health.

The activities I've listed in my table have often been things I've been trying to do for a long time, so hopefully writing them down will add more commitment to them.

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) Taha Wh훮nau (Family Wellbeing) Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)
Going for a run or walk once a day Calling a family member daily doing at least one meditation on the headspace app express gratitude for where I am each morning Swim in the sea once a week

Although these are small activities I know sticking to them will do nothing but be positive for my life, which is really important for me to remember when I dont feel the drive to do anything.
