Learning plan

below are my answers to questions to formulate my learning plan.

20 January 2024

What is your long term goal or career pathway

My long-term goal is to simply find employment at a place I'm happy to be a part of. A large part of my adult life I have not had a full-time job, and its always made me have a very bad sense of self-worth. Through my depression I withdrew from life a lot which didnt help with job prospects, and left large gaps in my CV. My goal is to finally break forgiveness of my stagnation and get into the world as a productive, working, and happy person.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My strengths are creativity,teamwork and social intelligence. I'm very good at working as a team and using my social intelligence to see How people are really feeling, or what I need to change about myself to work better in this certain group/situation. My biggest limitation is my motivation, which stems from years of inactivity. Some days I find even the smallest things really challenging, and I do worry that this will happen during the course. I have a tendancy to give up and retreat when im feeling overwhelmed which im really trying to correct.

Another limitation to learning will be managing my depression throughout the course. Although I'm a lot better now than only a short-time ago, I still have a lot of the depressive tendancies; To only focus on the negative, feel that I'm not good enough, be scared of trying because I've convinced myself I'll already fail. Thoughts like this are extremely toxic to learning and its important I remember everyone is here to help me and I'm doing this course to get a positive outcome. Its ok if i struggle or feel overwhelmed as thats completely natural, I just need to work hard to make sure it doesnt control me.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would love to see my routine,motivation, and just the ability to work hard improve. All of these are important to being an adult and I just havnt developed them enough due to my past. I know 3 months is a very short time but If i can at least get these skills going during study I will be in a good place to keep them going.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I will manage my workload productively by actively participating and being present at team meetings and classes. I will always do my best to pay attention and be present during school hours, and conduct myself proffesionally with staff and students alike. Its important to me to not cause distress to anyone by either what I say or how I act, and I intend everyone to feel safe around me. When things are becoming stressfull, or work is building up and starting to get to me I will communicate that with my co-ordinator and follow their advice, as well as keep working and not give up on myself!

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will make sure to actively ask for help when I need it from the tutors and my other students. Depending on the help required will dictate who I ask first. For smaller problems such as not being able to debug a piece of code, I will first talk to other students, but for bigger problems such as falling behind or becoming overwhelmed I will seek help from the tutors.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I expect the facilitation team to honestly listen when I have a problem, and do their best to help. i dont expect to be personally guided to find answers but i do expect honest help that can lead me to the correct answers. Another expectation I have is that their will be understanding during times of extreme stress, although it might not happen to me it is nice to think that the staff will be able to help someone find a way through their struggles, and give them some steps to continue working.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things

I currently do not have any other commitments, the only thing I have that can interfere with learning is I am planning to move house during February, which could take two days meaning I may fall behind temporarily.
